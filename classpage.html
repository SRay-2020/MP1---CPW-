Our Classes

This space is a showcase for our upcoming classes in 2021. All classes are subject to booking 14 days in advance and are dependent on current COVID-19 restrictions. We are very flexible with the dates so rescheduling is never a problem!

Beginners Class


A place for everyone to come and work some clay no matter what your level! Join us on this exciting two day course where you you will learn the basics of working clay in a fun and educational enviroment. On this course we will look at the history of clay in Ireland and how it developed to the craft that we know today. We will look at rudimentary pottery techniques and you will create and glaze your very first piece of pottery!


A course for someone compltely new to pottery!

Intermediate Class


A place for potters with some previous expereince to further develop their skills. Join us on this exciting two day course where you you will learn some interetsing techniques to further your skill set in a focused and educational enviroment. On this course we will look at some new crafting techniques and some exciting glazing methods. On this course you will mix and create your own glazes and test it out on a batch of your own pottery!


The course for the hobbyist potter in your life!

Check availabilty

Advanced Class


This course is for those who have worked with pottery for some years and know their way around the wheel. This intense 2 day course will let you elavate your skill set and learn and share your techniques with our resident potters. We will create difficult glazes and help you hone your craft even further so you are creating work to a professional standard.


A course for pro potters!
